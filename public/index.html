About this Tool

This is the Guest Guide Intelligence Scanner — a self-contained application that runs in any web browser without an internet connection to this chat. Open the HTML file, and the full workflow is available immediately.

The scanner queries live web sources on every run — casting across 80+ sources including EU institutions, national tourism bodies from all 27 EU member states plus UK and Switzerland, consumer magazines (Condé Nast, NatGeo, Geo Magazin, Le Figaro Voyage, Corriere Viaggi), market intelligence firms (IBISWorld, Euromonitor, WTTC, McKinsey), Nordic tourism bodies, rural and LEADER programme sources, spatial intelligence sources, and advocacy networks. Results are current, specific, and dated. The three-step Article Writer produces LinkedIn-optimised articles at Year-11 reading level, 1,000–1,200 words, with photorealistic image prompts and a LinkedIn précis. Parameters are baked in — never re-enter them.

Workflow: Run a scan → expand a signal → click an article idea → brief is generated → choose a headline → article is written. Every step happens inside this window.
